E-Commerce Sales & Customer Analytics

## 📌 Objective
Analyze retail sales data to identify revenue trends, customer behavior, and profit drivers using SQL, Python, and Power BI.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- SQL (Data Analysis)
- Python (Pandas, Matplotlib)
- Power BI (Dashboard)
- GitHub

---

## 📂 Dataset
- Superstore Sales Dataset
- Contains orders, customers, products, sales, and profit data

---

## 📊 Key Analysis Performed

### 🔹 Sales Analysis
- Total revenue calculation
- Monthly revenue trend
- Region-wise performance

### 🔹 Customer Analysis
- Top customers by revenue
- Repeat customer identification
- RFM segmentation

### 🔹 Product Analysis
- Top-selling products
- Category-wise profit analysis

---

## 🔥 Key Insights
- Revenue shows consistent growth with seasonal peaks
- Top customers contribute a significant share of revenue
- Certain categories generate high sales but low profit
- Repeat customers drive long-term business value
- Regional sales performance varies significantly

---

## 📈 Dashboard (Power BI)
- Sales Overview (Revenue, Orders, AOV)
- Customer Insights (Top Customers, Segmentation)
- Product Analysis (Top Products, Profit)
- Regional Performance Map
